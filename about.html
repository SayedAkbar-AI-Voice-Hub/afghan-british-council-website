Who We Are

Bridging the gap safely, supporting refugees, and nurturing a powerful professional diaspora.

Our History

Building on the foundation laid down by the initiation of Capacity for Afghanistan (C4A) in 2017, the Afghan British Council embodies a powerful transformation.

Our goal is to transcend simple relief. We construct an end-to-end framework where refugees are integrated into academic, social, and economic structures seamlessly. Our initiatives include delivering certified courses in Cross-Cultural Awareness, Food Hygiene, and tailoring paths that foster independence.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish a well-integrated, globally connected, and intellectually robust Afghan community in the UK, preserving cultural heritage while adopting cutting-edge progress.

Our Mission

To act as a central pillar for systemic change, combating "brain waste" among skilled immigrants and uniting diverse professionals within our database of leading specialists.
