Social & Cultural Events

Celebrating and preserving Afghan heritage through vibrant community gatherings, exhibitions, and cultural festivals across the UK.

Culture Is Our Strength

Afghanistan has one of the richest cultural heritages in the world. Through our social and cultural events, ABC creates spaces where that heritage lives — where language, music, food, and tradition are celebrated and passed to the next generation.

From Nowruz celebrations to community iftar dinners, cultural exhibitions, and Eid gatherings — we bring thousands of Afghans together across the UK each year.

Our Annual Events

Nowruz Celebration

Our flagship Afghan New Year celebration brings together hundreds of community members for food, music, poetry, and cultural performances.

Eid Gatherings

Community Eid dinners across London and beyond, creating space for Afghan families to celebrate together.

Cultural Exhibitions

Art, photography, and heritage exhibitions showcasing Afghanistan's history and the journey of the Afghan community in the UK.

Music & Arts

Concerts, poetry evenings, and performing arts showcasing Afghan talent — from classical music to contemporary Afghan artists.

Food & Community

Community iftars, Afghan food markets, and cultural lunches that bring families and professionals together around shared heritage.

Advocacy Events

Panel discussions, conferences, and public events on Afghan affairs — bringing community voices into public and political discourse.

Never Miss an Event

Members get priority access and discounted tickets to all ABC social and cultural events across the UK.
