Who We Are

Established in 2017 as Capacity for Afghanistan (C4A), the Afghan British Council (ABC) is a platform initiated by highly educated and talented Afghans with the aim to help all aspects of Afghan life.

We are dedicated to bridging cultural gaps and empowering Afghan professionals to contribute to global development.

Our Vision

To see a thriving, integrated Afghan community in the UK that remains connected to and capable of rebuilding Afghanistan.

Our Mission

Providing a comprehensive platform for education, cultural orientation, and professional networking to facilitate seamless integration.

Programs & Initiatives

Exhibitions & Advocacy

Partnering with prestigious UK universities to showcase Afghan heritage. Representing authentic voices in TV debates and documentaries.

Girls' Education Campaign

Providing critical financial assistance and educational continuity for Afghan girls facing barriers. Offering Pashto and Dari classes to preserve heritage.

Global Engagement

Engaging with UN representatives & UNITAR for sustainable resource mobilization and hosting Afghan-UK-USA-China networking events.

Strategic Research & Think Tank

Our research-driven advocacy shapes policies that protect refugee rights and promote sustainable development.

  • Strategic Research: The C4A Think Tank conducts in-depth analysis on refugee rights and bridges academic research with community needs.
  • Policy Recommendations: We provide expert insights to government bodies and international organizations.
  • Advocacy & Innovation: Driving impactful change through evidence-based long-term policy frameworks.
