A Heritage of Resilience.
A Future of Promise.

Established in 2017 as Capacity for Afghanistan (C4A), the Afghan British Council (ABC) serves as the leading intellectual and cultural platform for Afghans in the UK.

We bridge the gap between arriving refugees and established professionals, preventing "brain waste" and ensuring every individual can contribute their maximum potential to British society while honoring their Afghan roots.
