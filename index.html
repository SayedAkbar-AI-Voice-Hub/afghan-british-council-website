A Thriving Afghan Community
In the Heart of Britain

Fostering integration through education, professional networking, social & cultural events — while preserving our rich Afghan heritage and building tomorrow's Afghanistan today.

Discover Our Mission Join the Community

A Heritage of Resilience.
A Future of Promise.

The Afghan British Council (ABC) is a platform dedicated to supporting all aspects of the Afghan community in the UK — from newly arrived refugees to established academics and professionals.

Founded in 2017 as Capacity for Afghanistan (C4A), we envision a thriving Afghan community that contributes meaningfully to British society today and plays a vital role in rebuilding Afghanistan tomorrow.

What We Do

Four pillars that define our mission to empower every Afghan in the UK.

Education & Skills

Delivering certified courses, English tuition, and educational pathways that unlock independence and career progression.

Professional Networking

Connecting Afghan professionals, academics, and entrepreneurs across the UK to prevent brain waste and maximise community potential.

Social & Cultural Events

Celebrating and preserving Afghan heritage through vibrant community gatherings, exhibitions, and cultural festivals across the UK.
